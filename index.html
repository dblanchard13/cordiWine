Our Story

Cordi Winery is the first in Sutter County. When Mother Nature toppled 65% of the almond trees previously planted on the property, the Cordi's decided to make a dream come true. The first vines were planted in June 2009 and wine made from the first harvest of those vines received a bronze award in the 2013 San Francisco Chronicle Wine Competition.
